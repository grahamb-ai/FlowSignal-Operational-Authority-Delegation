Why Growth Stalls

    
As organisations grow, founders must delegate authority. Most do not hold back because they want control for its own sake. They hold back because they cannot confidently determine whether decisions remain within approved authority, policy and risk boundaries.

    
FlowSignal™ validates authority at the execution boundary. That means the business can move faster while preserving governance, accountability and defensibility.
