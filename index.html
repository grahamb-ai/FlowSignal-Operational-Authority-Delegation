Why This Matters

    
Authority and admissibility are not the same thing. A manager may have authority to approve spend, but the organisation may still be unable to trade with a supplier, execute in a jurisdiction, or act after a regulatory update.

    
Version 3 demonstrates the wider FlowSignal category: runtime authority infrastructure for consequential action, whether the actor is a person, a workflow, or an AI agent.
